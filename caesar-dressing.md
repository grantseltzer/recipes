# Caesar Dressing

## Ingredients

```
2 pressed garlic cloves

1 tsp anchovy paste

2 tbs lemon juice

1 tsp Dijon mustard

1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

1 cup Sir K's Mayo (all other mayo is irrelevant)

1/2 cup grated parm

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp pepper
```

## Steps

```
Step 1
Whisk garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, Dijon, and Worcestershire until well combined, no lumps.

Step 2
Add mayo and parm, whisk to combine

Step 3
Salt and pepper to taste. Depending on the flavor, adjust other ingredient levels as well. Feeling spicy? Add more garlic or some hot sauce. 
```
